# MoodTune Manual Testing Checklist

This checklist is used to manually verify all features of MoodTune, including setup, AI generation, playback, fallbacks, playlist management, selfie detection, real-time sharing, and edge cases.

---

## 1. SETUP
- [ ] **Server Launch**: Start the server using `python manage.py runserver` (or `python manage.py runserver 8001`). Verify it starts up without errors.
- [ ] **Console Logs**: Check the console for any `FutureWarning` or deprecation warnings. There should be none.
- [ ] **Environment Variables**: Verify that `GEMINI_API_KEY`, `YOUTUBE_API_KEY`, and optional `GCS_BUCKET_NAME` are loaded correctly from the `.env` file (errors should be logged/raised on startup if missing).

## 2. AI PLAYLIST GENERATION
- [ ] **Modal Toggle**: Click the "AI Playlist" button in the sidebar. Verify the modal opens smoothly.
- [ ] **Emoji Selection**: Click on any emoji mood in the grid. Verify the selection updates visually.
- [ ] **Custom Mood Text**: Type a custom mood description in the text input box.
- [ ] **Loading Overlay**: Click the "Generate" button. Verify the loading overlay shows up and transitions through the following states:
  - `Reading your vibe...`
  - `Finding your songs...`
  - `Verifying songs...`
  - `Almost ready...`
- [ ] **Playlist Content**: Verify that the playlist appears with exactly 8 songs.
- [ ] **Vibe Analysis**: Verify that the mood analysis text displays at the top of the tracklist.
- [ ] **Playlist Name**: Verify that the playlist name generated by AI is creative and relevant to the selected mood.

## 3. SONG PLAYBACK
- [ ] **Immediate Play**: Click any song in the playlist queue. Verify that it starts playing immediately.
- [ ] **Player Bar Info**: Verify that the bottom player bar updates with the correct song title, artist, and thumbnail.
- [ ] **Play/Pause controls**: Click the Play/Pause button in the player bar. Verify the track pauses/resumes correctly.
- [ ] **Next/Prev controls**: Click the Next/Previous buttons. Verify that it changes the active track accordingly.
- [ ] **Progress Bar**: Verify the progress slider matches the song duration and updates live every second.
- [ ] **Volume Controls**: Drag the volume slider. Verify that the audio level changes.
- [ ] **Playback Seeking**: Click at different positions on the progress slider. Verify the player seeks to that position.

## 4. UNAVAILABLE SONG HANDLING
- [ ] **Trigger Fallback**: Simulate or play a song with embedding restrictions (error 150/101).
- [ ] **Fallback Notification**: Verify that a red-bordered toast notification appears stating `Can't play in app` along with the song name and thumbnail.
- [ ] **YouTube Music Button**: Click the green "Open in YT Music" button. Verify that a new browser tab opens searching for the song on YouTube Music.
- [ ] **Auto-Advance Timer**: Do not click anything else. Verify that after exactly 4 seconds, the toast dismisses, the unplayable song is removed from the active playlist UI, and the player automatically advances to the next track.
- [ ] **Queue Stability**: Verify that skipping unplayable tracks does not crash or break the playlist queue.

## 5. SEARCH FEATURE
- [ ] **Search Box Interaction**: Switch to Search view, type a query into the main search box (e.g. "Taylor Swift"). Verify search results appear within 1-2 seconds.
- [ ] **Direct Play**: Click a search result. Verify it plays immediately.
- [ ] **Add to Playlist**: Hover over a search result and click the '+' button to add it to a playlist. Verify that it is appended to the selected playlist.

## 6. PLAYLIST MANAGEMENT
- [ ] **Create Playlist**: Click the "+" button in the playlists sidebar section. Enter a name and press Enter. Verify the new playlist appears.
- [ ] **Add Song**: Add songs from Search results. Verify the song count next to the playlist name incremented.
- [ ] **Remove Song**: Hover over a track in the playlist view and click the trash/remove icon. Verify the song is removed from the list.
- [ ] **Delete Playlist**: Right-click the playlist in the sidebar and click "Delete Playlist". Verify it is removed.
- [ ] **Persistence**: Refresh the page. Verify all created playlists and their songs persist.

## 7. SELFIE MOOD DETECTION
- [ ] **Detect Modal**: Click the "🤳 Detect My Mood" button in the sidebar. Verify the camera modal opens.
- [ ] **Camera Frame**: Verify that your camera feed is rendered inside a circular frame.
- [ ] **Capture Countdown**: Click "Capture". Verify the `3-2-1` countdown displays overlaying the camera feed.
- [ ] **Flash Effect**: Verify a flash animation occurs when the photo is taken.
- [ ] **Mood Result**: Verify the detected mood text and confidence percentage display (e.g. `Sad — 87% confident`).
- [ ] **Auto-Generation**: Verify that the app automatically dismisses the modal and starts generating a playlist based on the detected mood.

## 8. REAL-TIME LISTENING ROOM
- [ ] **Create Room**: Click "Create Room" in the Listening Room sidebar section. Verify that a 6-character code is generated, and the page URL reloads with `?room=CODE`.
- [ ] **Sidebar Room Display**: Verify that the generated room code is displayed in the sidebar.
- [ ] **Copy Share Link**: Click "Copy Share Link". Verify the correct URL is copied to your clipboard.
- [ ] **Join Room**: Open a second browser tab (or incognito window) and paste the copied URL. Verify the second tab joins the same room.
- [ ] **Live Indicator**: Verify that the status indicator dot turns green and shows "🟢 Live" on both tabs.
- [ ] **Play Sync**: Play a song in Tab 1. Verify that Tab 2 updates to play the same song within 1-2 seconds.
- [ ] **Pause Sync**: Pause the song in Tab 1. Verify Tab 2 pauses.
- [ ] **Leave Room**: Click "Leave Room" in Tab 1. Verify it disconnects, returns to solo mode, and the indicator dot turns red ("Offline").

## 9. EDGE CASES
- [ ] **Page Refresh**: Refresh the page while a song is playing. Verify the app reloads cleanly without crash loops.
- [ ] **Dismiss Modals**: Open AI or Selfie modals, then click outside or hit the 'x' button. Verify they close without errors.
- [ ] **Rapid Clicking**: Click the next/prev or play buttons rapidly multiple times. Verify the player stays in a valid state.
- [ ] **Empty Search**: Submit an empty query or spaces in the search bar. Verify it is handled gracefully (returns no results or clears the pane).
- [ ] **API Failures**: Temporarily disable the internet or modify API keys. Verify that the app displays a toast error notification rather than crashing or showing a blank page.
